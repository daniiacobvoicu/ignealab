Infrastructure

Our Lab hosts the McGill Cell Factory Facility, established as a yeast dedicated platform and recently upgraded to other bioproduction chasses. The infrastructure is funded by CFI, NSERC & CIHR grants, as well as having been acquired by the McGill iGEM team.
