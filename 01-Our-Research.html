Our Research

Synthetic Biology Metabolic Engineering Sustainability Cell Factories Terpenoid Biosynthesis High-Value Compounds Chemical Diversity Biological Membrane Engineering BioManufacturing

Our research program aims to engineer biosynthetic systems for production of high value compounds with a broad range of applications in medicine, industry or agriculture through Synthetic Biology and Metabolic Engineering approaches. Our group applies innovative approaches by biomimicking natural systems to redesign the metabolism of production hosts to improve performance of heterologous pathways and enzymes, promote pathway regulation and optimization by triggering host natural evolution that will ultimately accelerate discovery of new pathways and assemble new functional synthetic biosynthetic routes. Our lab focuses on two major themes: A. Development of approaches and tools and B. Biosynthesis and production of small molecules.
