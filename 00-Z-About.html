Our mission is enable sustainable living through harnessing the power of biological synthesis

We are a Metabolic Engineering lab applying Synthetic Biology principles for production of high-value compounds in heterologous hosts. Targeting natural products from the terpenoid class, we endeavor to discover novel biological activities by exploring nature’s chemical space, engineer enzyme catalytic activities for tailored synthesis and optimize metabolic fluxes for high yields. We use yeast as the chassis of choice to move on precision fermentation from lab bench to industry scale. Thus, under the Bioengineering overarching umbrella, we embark on an interdisciplinary research aiming to provide solutions to real world problems in medicine, industry or agriculture.
